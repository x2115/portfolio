About Me

Henry Haak is a game developer from Albuquerque, New Mexico. He's been designing and programming games for over 15 years.

Henry has a BA in Digital Media with a focus on Gaming from the University of New Mexico in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Before attending UNM, Henry worked in IT at Perinatal Associates of New Mexico, and developed software for the company's employees as well as the IT department specifically.

Henry has made games for a variety of genres, platforms, and engines, and loves to experiment with game design. Additionally, he also enjoys programming games and seeing everything come together- it's like magic!

He is looking for a role where his skills as a designer or programmer (or both at once!) can be put to good use.
